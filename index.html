Whether you simply have a product concept or are looking to incorporate a select list of ingredients and functions, we can work with you to develop a paper formulation that will achieve your objectives. Our Formulation Development services include all the major components required to make a market ready consumer product including Cosmetic Actives, Emulsification Systems, Preservation Systems and Fragrances for the following product formats: Formulation Development services include detailed steps required for the manufacture of your cosmetic product.

Ingredients and Raw Material Sourcing


We have extensive knowledge of the suppliers of cosmetics and personal care products ingredients that will meet all statutory requirements. We can connect you with suppliers that can provide quality ingredients as per your development capacity and budget. You will also be supplied with ingredient Material Safety Data Sheets and certifications for each and every ingredient in your formulation.

Prototype Development


Once your formulation is complete, our lab will provide you with a 500 gram prototype product sample for your evaluation of product aesthetics (color, viscosity, odor/fragrance) and sensorials (how the product feels when applied to the skin). If necessary, we can then adjust the formulation to comply with your market objectives.

Stability and Preservative Challenge Testing


A critical and necessary component of any formulation development project, we will conduct preliminary in-house Stability and Microbial Testing. Following our in-house testing, your product prototype will be submitted to independent laboratories that specialize in independent third-party testing of cosmetic formulations.
